Pokémon Trading Card Game (GBC) - Promo Card Guide

A heavy work-in-progress.

In PTCG (GBC) there are 20 promotional cards that are not obtainable via packs, and many of them can be missed by losing matches you might not have expected to come up. This is a guide containing all promo cards, and how they are acquired, so that you can be prepared. All of the promotional cards are listed in the order they appear in the album.
