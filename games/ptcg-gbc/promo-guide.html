Pokémon Trading Card Game (GBC) - Promo Card Guide

A heavy work-in-progress.

In PTCG (GBC) there are 20 promotional cards that are not obtainable via packs. This is guide will list all promo cards and how they are acquired so that you can be prepared. All of the promotional cards are listed in the order that they appear in the album.

Set # Pokémon Acquisition Information
P01 Arcanine Lv34 Trade a Lapras Lv31 to the Gal in the Water Club break room.
P09 Electabuzz Lv20 Trade an Electabuzz Lv 35 to the Chap located in the Lightning Club break room.
P11 Slowpoke Lv9 After collecting enough energies, a Lad in the upper side of the Fire Club break room will ask you to give him all of your unused energy cards. If you agree he will inform you that this promo card is hidden on the wall, two tiles left of the bookcase. If you refuse he will become angry, and leave. It is recommended that you put all your energy cards in decks before speaking to him. This will allow you to keep your energies while still receiving the information.
P12 Mewtwo Lv60 After winning your third medal the first Challenge Cup will be held at the Challenge Hall. This card is the prize if you are able to win all three consecutive battles.
P13 Mewtwo Lv60 In the break room of the Psychic Club there is an NPC named Pappy. Speak with him and agree that you will defeat Murray, the Psychic Club Master. After you have obtained the Psychic Club medal, show him your medal and you will receive this promo.
P14 Mew Lv8 After receiving your sixth medal you can return to the second Challenge Cup. If you are able to win the three consecutive battles this card will be your prize.
P15 Jigglypuff Lv12 After obtaining your second medal a scripted event occurs when you enter the club lobby. Ronald will appear and challenge you to a battle, and if you are able to win you will obtain this promo.
P17 Imakuni? This card is obtainable by defeating Imakuni? your third and sixth time.
P18 Super Energy Retrieval Just like the event for your second medal, after you receive your sixth enter the club lobby will trigger an event where Ronald challenges you to a battle. Winning earns you this card.
PXX Venusaur Lv 64 This card was intended to be obtainable via Card Pop!, but due to a programming error this card is not obtainable without cheats, glitches or save editing.
PXX Mew Lv15 Low odds to receive this card via Card Pop!

The Challenge Cup

If you are unfortunate enough to miss the opportunity to acquire one of the promotional cards during your play-through, there is another way to acquire them. After defeating the the Grand Masters at the Pokémon Dome, each time the game's save file is loaded, provided you are not in the Challenge Hall, there is a 25% chance that the Challenge Cup becomes available. The prize for winning the Challenge Cup is a random promo card.

Note: The Legendary cards (Moltres, Articuno, Zapdos, Dragonite), Phantom cards (Venusaur and Mew Lv15), and Imakuni? are not a part of the Challenge Cup Prize pool.
