Pokémon Trading Card Game (GBC) - Card List

The following is a list of the 228 card collection for the Pokémon Trading Card Game for Game Boy Color. These cards are be sorted by their packs, and set numbers, as recorded in the in-game card gallery. This list should prove helpful for any individual looking to expand/complete their collection, by showing what packs they may need to farm.

Note:
You can mouse over a Pokémon's attacks, or Pokémon Powers, to see the details. Eventually, you will be able to mouse over the Set # to see the in-game images of the cards.
