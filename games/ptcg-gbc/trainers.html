A heavy work-in-progress.

A collection of data for the static trainers in the game. Useful for finding a NPC you can easily farm. Primary goal is to document each trainer's pack reward, and then potentially expand from there to include other information, such as deck lists. The locations and trainer names are in alphabetical order.

Scripted battles and battles that only happen once are not included on this page. These matches are often for a promotional card, so any information about those battles would be found in the Promo Card Guide.

Other Trainers

Imakuni?

While technically not static, Imakuni? is another farmable trainer. He first begins to appear after speaking to the Lass in the center of the Water Club break room. It is from that point that Imakuni? appears in the Fighting, Lightning, Science or Water Club break room each time the save file is loaded.

There are two different types of rewards for defeating Imakuni?. The first, and most notable, reward for defeating Imakuni? is one of each pack of cards: Colosseum, Evolution, Mystery and Laboratory. The other reward is the Imakuni? promotional card, which is received after winning your third and sixth match against him.
