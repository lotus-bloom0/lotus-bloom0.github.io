Pokémon Franchise Bookmarks

General Bookmarks

These are some of the best Pokémon-centric websites around. A lot of the bookmarks that come after this section may just be links to different parts of one of these websites.

Bulbapedia, the Pokemon Encyclopedia

The Wiki for all things Pokémon.

The Official Pokémon Website

Obligatory offical website shoe-in.

Serebii.net

The God-King of Pokémon news outlets. If you're looking for news regarding any Pokémon IP this is the first place you should go. They also have a top tier database for each IP. Game information, anime info, special events, release schedules, you name it. Serebii celebrates it's 25th anniversary this year (2024)!

Tools for Competitive Play

Pikalytics

Pokemon Showdown, Tournament and Battle Stadium usage analytics.

Pokemon Showdown

An extremely popular, unoffical battle simulator. You can play with either competitive format rules, or community balanced rule formats.

  • Damage Calculator

    Run damage calulations quickly. A must-use for determining how you want to EV train your team.

Serebii's Natures Chart

Forgot which nature boosts the stats you want? No problem.

Smogon University

Referred to as "the Competitive Dex." The site is a lot more focused on getting you quick answers to what a Pokémon's stats, and move pool look like for both, official and Pokémon Showdown rule-sets.

Covers Pokemon X/Y and to current.
